Get To Know Us!

Welcome to Marcus Inigo Bakery on Mastrili Street, Bambang, Taguig City! Savor a diverse selection of pastries, from the rich Ensemada to the sweet notes of Pandecoco and Pandeube. Explore the flaky delights of Hopia and Spanish Bread, or indulge in the comforting goodness of Banana Bread and Pudding. Our signature Pandesal stands as a testament to our commitment to artisanal perfection. Join us at our charming location for an exquisite taste of crafted delights.

Marie Magpantay is the owner of the bakery, which opened four years ago. Pandesal was the only bread sold at first, but as time went on, new varieties of bread were added.
